How to use this directory

Each card below identifies a person or institution who can provide data for one or more missing dimensions. Cards include contact info, the specific data we need, why this contact is relevant, and a draft outreach message you can copy and customize. Click any card to expand it. Use the filters to focus on a specific gap area.
